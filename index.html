ABOUT US

The Premier Health Group (PHG) is a human services agency built on the core values of excellence and innovation, compassion and integrity, diversity and teamwork, dignity and respect.

Through its subsidiaries, PHG is committed to providing each patient with timely medical care, humane treatment and person-centered supports to assist them in gaining independence and pursuing optimal recovery at home and in the community. PHG through its subsidiaries aims to provide comprehensive, high quality, multidisciplinary and compassionate care to all patients served within the comfort of their various locations.

Through its subsidiaries, PHG’s goal is to create a continuum of care from pediatric home health services to hospice services, utilizing an efficient model across services. PHG seeks to provide quality healthcare through a coordinated plan of nursing, ancillary and rehabilitative care. While prioritizing providing each individual and family with education and advocacy opportunities to maximize the service needs of each patient.
